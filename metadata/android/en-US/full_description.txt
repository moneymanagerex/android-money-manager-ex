<p>A simple and easy-to-use application to manage personal finances, bank accounts, family budget, and more. The main goal is to provide a mobile implementation for an application we love to use on our desktop, Money Manager EX.</p>

<b>Features</b>

<ul>
  <li>Management of single transactions</li>
  <li>Account, payee, category, and currency management</li>
  <li>Cloud storage-based synchronization, including:
    <ul>
      <li>Cloud storage such as Google Drive and Nextcloud</li>
      <li>Sync services like Syncthing</li>
    </ul>
  </li>
  <li>Support for SQLCipher encryption</li>
  <li>Compatibility with Desktop MMEX</li>
  <li>Repeating transactions</li>
  <li>Charts for reports</li>
</ul>
