<p>A simple and easy-to-use application to manage personal finances, bank accounts, family budget, and more. The main goal is to provide a mobile implementation for an application we love to use on our desktop, Money Manager EX.</p>

<b>Features</b>

- Management of single transactions<br>
- Account, payee, category, and currency management<br>
- Cloud storage-based synchronization, including:<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Cloud storage such as Google Drive and Nextcloud<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;- Sync services like Syncthing<br>
- Support for SQLCipher encryption<br>
- Compatibility with Desktop MMEX<br>
- Repeating transactions<br>
- Charts for reports<br>
