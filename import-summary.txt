Moved Files:

Eclipse projects. Here's how the projects were restructured:

* AndroidManifest.xml => androidSVG_12\src\main\AndroidManifest.xml
* res\ => androidSVG_12\src\main\res\
* src\ => androidSVG_12\src\main\java\
